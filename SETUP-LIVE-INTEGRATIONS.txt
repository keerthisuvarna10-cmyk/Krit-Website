To enable live Firebase Google login and customer storage:
Fill window.KRIT_ECOMMERCE.firebase inside the HTML near the end of the file.
Required fields:
- apiKey
- authDomain
- projectId
- storageBucket
- messagingSenderId
- appId
- measurementId (optional)

To enable Meta / Facebook Pixel:
Fill window.KRIT_ECOMMERCE.facebookPixelId in the same config block.
